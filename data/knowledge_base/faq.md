# Acme SaaS Inc. — Frequently Asked Questions

## What does Acme SaaS do?
Acme SaaS Inc. provides a cloud-based project management and analytics platform
for small and medium-sized businesses. Our plans are Starter, Growth, and
Enterprise.

## How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot password". Enter your account email and
we'll send a reset link that is valid for 60 minutes. If you don't receive it
within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

## How do I contact support?
You can reach support by email at support@acmesaas.example or through the live
chat widget in the bottom-right of the dashboard. Support hours are Monday to
Friday, 9am–6pm Eastern Time.

## Where can I find my invoices?
All invoices are available under Settings → Billing → Invoices. You can download
any invoice as a PDF.

## Do you offer a free trial?
Yes. Every new account gets a 14-day free trial of the Growth plan. No credit
card is required to start the trial.

## Is my data secure?
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We are SOC 2
Type II certified and run daily encrypted backups with 30-day retention.
