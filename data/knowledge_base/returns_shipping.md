# Acme Store — Returns & Shipping Policy

Acme also ships branded hardware (laptop stands, webcams, and accessories)
through the Acme Store.

## Shipping
- Standard shipping (3–5 business days) is free on orders over $50.
- Express shipping (1–2 business days) is $12.
- Orders placed before 2pm ET ship the same business day.
- We currently ship within the United States and Canada only.

## Tracking
A tracking number is emailed when your order ships. You can also find it under
Orders → Order details.

## Returns
You may return unused items in their original packaging within 30 days of
delivery for a full refund. Items must be in resaleable condition.

To start a return, go to Orders → Return item, or email returns@acmestore.example
with your order number. We'll send a prepaid return label.

## Damaged or wrong items
If an item arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 7 days with a photo.
We'll ship a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund, including shipping.

## Refund timing
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are issued to the
original payment method within 5–10 business days.
