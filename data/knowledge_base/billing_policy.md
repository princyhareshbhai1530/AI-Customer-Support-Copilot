# Acme SaaS Inc. — Billing & Refund Policy

## Plans and pricing
- **Starter:** $19 / user / month, billed monthly or annually.
- **Growth:** $49 / user / month, includes advanced analytics and integrations.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing with SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated CSM.

Annual billing receives a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.

## Billing cycle
Subscriptions renew automatically on the same calendar day each cycle. You can
view your next renewal date under Settings → Billing.

## Refund policy
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers on the Starter
and Growth plans. To request a refund within 30 days of your first payment,
contact billing@acmesaas.example with your account email and invoice number.
Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Renewal payments (after the first billing cycle) are non-refundable, but you can
cancel at any time to stop future charges.

## How to cancel
Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription. Your plan remains active until
the end of the current paid period; you will not be charged again after that.

## Failed payments
If a payment fails, we retry three times over seven days. If all retries fail,
the account is downgraded to read-only until billing is updated.
